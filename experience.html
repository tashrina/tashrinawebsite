My Experience

Volunteering for vaccination program called
National COVID-19 Immunisation Programme (PICK) .



When the pandemic hits the world, I played a part in joining for volunteer under MyVac.
It has been a new experience for me since not everyone can have the opportunity as mine.
I have been faced with a lot of people behavior such as they demand to change the type of vaccine, the old folks didnt know how to use mySejahtera etc.
For that, I have exposed myself to an environment that require communication, it helps me to become more confident since I am an introvert person.
Moreover, it includes decision making when in a situation of pressure.
